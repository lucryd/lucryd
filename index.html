Services

Program Assessment

Using our team's extensive experience and our proprietary assessment materials we can evaluate your current program and provide an action plan with tailored improvement activities.

Management

Our experienced team can take over the day-to-day management of your cardiopulmonary rehab program, reducing your costs and increasing productivity.

Technology

We've built a comprehensive platform for cardiopulmonary rehab programs to optimize operations and improve the patient experience.

Our process

We tailor our approach to each program by following these steps.

  • Step 1

    Program assessment

    We start by assessing your program in four main categories: 1) Referral; 2) Recruitment; 3) Operations; and 4) Billing

  • Step 2

    Action plan

    Based on the results of our program assessment, we create a custom action plan that contains performance improvement activities in each of the four main categories.

  • Step 3

    Implementation

    Our team helps you implement your custom action plan as well as provides access to our technology platform to assist with performance improvement activities and operations.

  • Step 4

    Management

    We'll never just give you policies and procedures and walk away! You'll have access to our team of experts to help manage the day-to-day operations of your program.
